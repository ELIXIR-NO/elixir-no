Sweden and SciLifeLab has launched a National Pathogen Portal to facilitate sharing of data related to pathogens and facilitate future pandemic preparedness. ELIXIR Norway has applied for national funding from the Norwegian Research Council to build a Norwegian Pathogen Portal.
