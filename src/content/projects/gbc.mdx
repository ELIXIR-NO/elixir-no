---
title: Global Biodata Coalition

---

The [Global Biodata Coalition (GBC)](https://globalbiodata.org/) aims to stabilise and ensure sustainable financial support for the global biodata infrastructure and in particular to identify for prioritized long-term support a set of Global Core Data Resources that are crucial for sustaining the broader biodata infrastructure.
<br/>
The Research Council of Norway and ELIXIR Norway are actively engaged in this work and our Head of Node, Professor Inge Jonassen, is represented on the interim steering board.
